Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Cancellation Policy explains how cancellations work on SkillPilot (skillspilot.xyz), including order cancellation, account cancellation, and service-related cancellations.

1. Business Model - No Subscriptions

SkillPilot operates on a one-time credit purchase model, NOT a subscription model. There are no recurring charges, auto-renewals, or subscription fees. You only pay when you choose to buy credits. There is nothing to "cancel" in terms of recurring payments.

2. Order Cancellation

Since credits are digital goods delivered instantly upon successful payment:

  • Orders cannot be cancelled after payment is successfully processed
  • If a payment fails or is interrupted mid-transaction, no credits will be added and no charge will be applied to your account
  • If you are charged but credits are not delivered, please refer to our Refund Policy

3. Cancellation Before Payment

You may cancel a purchase at any time before completing the payment on the Razorpay/Stripe checkout page. Simply close the payment window. No charges will be applied.

4. Account Cancellation / Deletion

You may request deletion of your SkillPilot account at any time. To do so:

  • Send an email to support@skillspilot.xyz from your registered email address
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered name and email address

Upon account deletion:

  • Your personal data will be removed within 30 days
  • All unused credits will be forfeited and cannot be refunded
  • Your generation history and saved content will be permanently deleted
  • This action is irreversible

5. Service Cancellation by SkillPilot

We reserve the right to cancel or suspend your access to the platform if:

  • You violate our Terms and Conditions
  • Fraudulent activity is detected on your account
  • You use VPN/proxy to manipulate regional pricing
  • You engage in abuse or misuse of the platform

In such cases, unused credits will be forfeited without refund.

6. Service Discontinuation

In the unlikely event that SkillPilot discontinues its services:

  • We will provide at least 30 days advance notice via email to all registered users
  • Users will be given a reasonable period to use their remaining credits
  • If credits cannot be utilized, proportional refunds may be offered at our discretion

7. Contact Us

For cancellation requests or questions about this policy:

Email: support@skillspilot.xyz

Website: https://skillspilot.xyz

Response Time: Within 48 hours on business days
