4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential cookies and browser local storage only for:

We do NOT use third-party tracking cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data we hold

  • Deletion - Request deletion of your account and associated data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@skillspilot.xyz.

7. Data Retention
