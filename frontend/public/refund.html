Refund Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which SkillPilot processes refunds for credit purchases made on our platform at skillspilot.xyz.

1. Nature of Purchase

SkillPilot sells digital credits that are delivered instantly upon successful payment. Credits are used to access AI-powered tools on the platform. Since credits are digital goods with immediate delivery, all purchases are generally considered final.

2. Refund Eligibility

We will process a refund in the following situations:

  • Duplicate Payment: You were charged more than once for the same order/transaction
  • Failed Delivery: Payment was successful but credits were not added to your account
  • Overcharge: You were charged an amount higher than the displayed price
  • Technical Failure: A platform error prevented you from using purchased credits within 24 hours of purchase, and the issue could not be resolved by our support team

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • Credits have already been used (fully or partially)
  • Dissatisfaction with the quality of AI-generated content
  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Failure to use credits before account termination due to terms violation
  • Price differences due to regional/currency variations
  • Account suspension or termination for violation of our Terms and Conditions

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to support@skillspilot.xyz with the following details:

  • Your registered email address
  • Razorpay/Stripe Transaction ID or Order ID
  • Date and time of purchase
  • Amount paid and currency
  • Detailed reason for the refund request
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

5. Refund Review Process

  • All refund requests are reviewed within 5-7 business days of receipt
  • You will receive an email notification with the outcome of your request
  • We may request additional information or evidence before processing

6. Refund Processing

  • Approved refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • Refund processing time: 7-10 business days after approval
  • The refund will be in the same currency as the original transaction (INR or USD)
  • Bank processing times may vary and are outside our control

7. Partial Refunds

If you have used some credits from a purchase, a partial refund may be issued for the unused portion at our discretion. The refund amount will be calculated proportionally based on the credits remaining.

8. Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with the refund decision, you may:

  • Reply to the refund decision email with additional details
  • Raise a dispute through your payment provider (Razorpay/Stripe)

9. Contact Us

For refund requests or payment-related queries:

Email: support@skillspilot.xyz

Website: https://skillspilot.xyz

Response Time: Within 48 hours on business days
