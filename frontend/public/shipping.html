Shipping and Exchange Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This policy explains how SkillPilot (skillspilot.xyz) handles delivery and exchange of purchased products.

1. Digital Product – No Physical Shipping

SkillPilot is a 100% digital platform. We sell digital credits that are used to access AI-powered tools. There are no physical goods, no physical shipping, and no delivery charges involved in any transaction.

2. Instant Digital Delivery

Upon successful payment, credits are delivered instantly to your SkillPilot account. There is no waiting period, shipping time, or manual processing. Your credit balance is updated immediately and you can start using the AI tools right away.

3. Delivery Confirmation

  • Credits are reflected in your account dashboard immediately after payment
  • A payment confirmation is displayed on-screen after successful transaction
  • You can verify your updated credit balance on the sidebar of any page

4. Failed Delivery

In the rare case that payment is successful but credits are not added to your account:

  • Wait 5 minutes and refresh your dashboard
  • If credits still don't appear, contact us at support@skillspilot.xyz with your transaction/order ID
  • We will investigate and resolve the issue within 48 hours
  • See our Refund Policy for eligible refund cases

5. Exchange Policy

Since SkillPilot sells digital credits (not physical products):

  • Credits cannot be exchanged for other products, services, or cash
  • Credits cannot be transferred to another user account
  • Credit packages cannot be swapped for a different package after purchase
  • If you purchased the wrong package, refer to our Refund Policy for options

6. No Returns

As credits are digital goods with instant delivery, they cannot be "returned." Once credits are added to your account, the delivery is considered complete. For payment issues, please see our Refund Policy.

7. Contact Us

For delivery issues or questions about this policy:

Email: support@skillspilot.xyz

Website: https://skillspilot.xyz

Response Time: Within 48 hours on business days
